About

A NSW construction company

Smith & Turner — on site

Smith & Turner Construction is a NSW-based construction company delivering high-quality building works for clients across Sydney and regional NSW.

With over a decade of industry experience behind our team, we've successfully delivered residential and commercial projects defined by meticulous attention to detail, quality workmanship, transparent communication, and dependable service.

Our approach

Founded to close that gap

Smith & Turner was founded to close that gap. Every project benefits from hands-on director involvement from start to finish, fair and justified quoting with no hidden surprises, proactive communication at every stage, and a quality-first approach to the finished result.
