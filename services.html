Our Services

What we deliver

Four disciplines, delivered to one standard, scoped properly, priced clearly, finished cleanly.

01

Residential construction

From concept to completion, we build quality homes designed around how you live. Our scope covers new home builds, knockdown-rebuilds, granny flats and secondary dwellings, structural work, and full project management including liaising with architects, certifiers, and trades. Every project is overseen directly by our director, ensuring consistent quality control and a single point of accountability from groundwork through to handover, including any waterproofing, rectification or finishing works needed to hand over a home that's right the first time.

02

Commercial construction

We deliver commercial builds on time and on budget, without compromising on quality. This includes new commercial structures, base-building works, structural alterations, shell-and-core construction, and retail and office fitouts for small to mid-sized projects. Our team coordinates engineering, certification, and trade scheduling, working around trading hours and centre timelines where needed, so the project moves through approvals and construction without unnecessary delays.

03

Internal & external renovations

Whether it's a single room refresh or a whole-of-home transformation, we bring the same attention to detail to every renovation. Internally, this covers kitchen and bathroom renovations, structural alterations, room additions, and full interior refits. Externally, we handle façade upgrades, decking, alfresco areas, cladding, roofing, waterproofing, defect rectification and exterior structural work. We sequence trades carefully to minimise disruption and keep your home liveable wherever possible throughout the works.

04

Project management

Every Smith & Turner project runs under hands-on, director-led project management, a single point of accountability from the first drawing to the final handover. We coordinate architects, engineers, certifiers and trades, manage the program and budget, and keep you informed at every stage with proactive communication and quality checks throughout. Whether we're delivering the build or overseeing your project on your behalf, you get fair, justified quoting with no hidden surprises and a finished result you can stand behind.
