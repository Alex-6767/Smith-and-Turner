About

A NSW construction company

Smith & Turner — drafted in 3D

Smith & Turner Construction is a NSW-based construction company delivering high-quality building works for clients across Sydney and regional NSW.

With over a decade of industry experience behind our team, we've successfully delivered residential and commercial projects defined by meticulous attention to detail, quality workmanship, transparent communication, and dependable service.

The problem we solve

Building, done differently

Our approach

Founded to close that gap

Smith & Turner was founded to close that gap. Every project benefits from hands-on director involvement from start to finish, fair and justified quoting with no hidden surprises, proactive communication at every stage, and a quality-first approach to the finished result.

Our Services

What we deliver

01

Residential construction

From concept to completion, we build quality homes designed around how you live. Our scope covers new home builds, knockdown-rebuilds, granny flats and secondary dwellings, structural work, and full project management including liaising with architects, certifiers, and trades. Every project is overseen directly by our director, ensuring consistent quality control and a single point of accountability from groundwork through to handover.

02

Commercial construction

We deliver commercial builds on time and on budget, without compromising on quality. This includes new commercial structures, base-building works, structural alterations, and shell-and-core construction for small to mid-sized projects. Our team coordinates engineering, certification, and trade scheduling — ensuring the project moves through approvals and construction without unnecessary delays.

03

Internal & external renovations

Whether it's a single room refresh or a whole-of-home transformation, we bring the same attention to detail to every renovation. Internally, this covers kitchen and bathroom renovations, structural alterations, room additions, and full interior refits. Externally, we handle façade upgrades, decking, alfresco areas, cladding, roofing, and exterior structural work. We sequence trades carefully to minimise disruption and keep your home liveable wherever possible throughout the works.

04

Remediation & rectification works

From defect rectification to general upkeep, we help homeowners and property managers protect their investment. Our scope includes but is not limited to waterproofing repairs, structural defect remediation, cladding and façade repairs, general building maintenance, and pre-sale or post-purchase rectification works. Every job starts with an honest, thorough assessment so you understand the cause of the issue and how it will be rectified.

05

Retail fitout

We help retail businesses bring their space to life, balancing functionality, brand presence, and customer flow. This covers shopfront construction, joinery and display installation, lighting and electrical coordination, flooring, and compliance works such as fire and accessibility requirements. We work around centre management timelines and trading hours to get you open with minimal downtime.

06

Office & workspace fitout

Modern, functional workspaces that support how your team works. Our scope includes partitioning, ceiling and flooring works, joinery, electrical and data coordination, breakout and meeting room construction, and base building compliance. We manage the fitout end-to-end — from layout planning through to final inspections — so your space is ready to move into on schedule.
